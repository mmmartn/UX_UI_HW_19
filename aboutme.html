About me?

RAYMOND MARTIN CARPIO

WHO?

I moved to California when I was 15 and received
my B.A. in Anthropology studying cultural sensitivity.
Since then I’ve thrived in the restaurant industry,
managing teams and delegating problems.

WHAT?

I’m currently based in Irvine, CA, studying under
UCI’s UX/UI Design program. I’m particularly
interested in designing for social and service
products.

WHY?

My journey into UX/UI design started with a humble
mobile app prototype. Since then my exposure to
the end-to-end process have left me with a passion
for design. As a designer, I’m continually learning
ways to understand and solve challenges through
technology that can make life easier, bit by bit.
